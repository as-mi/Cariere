P&G serves consumers around the world with one of the strongest portfolios of trusted, quality, leadership brands, including Always®, Ambi Pur®, Ariel®, Fairy®, Gillette®, head&shoulders®, Lenor®, Oral-B®, Pampers®, Pantene®, Tide®. Globally, our brands are trusted in millions of living rooms, kitchens, laundry rooms, and bathrooms—and have been passed down from generation to generation. Over the course of 181 years, they’ve challenged convention, led innovation, and helped shape culture.

Since our first steps into the country in 1994, when the company opened the first office in Bucharest, we have been on a growth journey together with the Romanian people through love brands which helped them take care of themselves, their families and homes with the power of the most leading technology and science.

Today, over 750 employees work in Romania, in the P&G Office, our state-of-the-art Plant in Urlati, Prahova, in the distribution center in Timisoara, and the innovative Global Business Service center in Bucharest, which supports 66 P&G other sites in 15 different languages.

Our Urlati plant is one of the most technologically advanced and environmentally friendly P&G Plants worldwide, serving over 1 billion consumers across over 40 markets in Europe, Turkey, Israel, Central Asia and the Caucasus Region.

All of this was possible because of our People who are our biggest asset. We have been growing the best talents in Romania for over 25 years, who pioneered and still lead FMCG industry development in the country. Giving people responsibility from day one, growing their knowledge and skills, promoting work – life integration. Our People are and always will be the foundation of our success.
